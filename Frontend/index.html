02 / 04 What's included
The full offer

Every site comes with a year of care, built in.

We don't just build your website and disappear. For the first year, we maintain it — every three months — at no extra cost.

01

Quarterly maintenance — 4 visits, free

Every three months for the first year, we go through your site: update software, check security, fix anything that broke, refresh content if needed.

02

Updates & security patches

CMS updates, plugin updates, security patches, dependency bumps. We handle the technical side so you don't have to think about it.

03

Content changes

Need to update a price, swap a photo, change your hours, add a new service? Send us the change and we'll make it.

04

Backups & monitoring

Your site is backed up regularly. We monitor uptime and get notified if something goes down. You don't have to check — we do.

05

One honest price

We quote you one number based on what you need. No tiers, no surprise add-ons. The year of maintenance is part of that number, not an extra.

After the first year: maintenance continues at a simple monthly rate. No lock-in, no contract — cancel anytime.
03 / 04 Process
How it works

From first email to live site in two to six weeks.

2–6 Weeks to launch
1 yr Free maintenance
4× Quarterly visits
Week 1

Understand

We talk about your business, your customers, and what you need the site to do. No questionnaires — just a conversation. We come back with a plan and a price.

Week 2 — 3

Design

We sketch the layout, choose typography and colors, and write the structure. You see work in progress and give feedback as it develops.

Week 4 — 5

Build

We develop the site, add your content, and test it across devices and browsers. You watch it come together on a private staging link from day one.

Week 6

Launch & hand off

We go live, set up your domain and email, and schedule your first quarterly maintenance visit. The site is yours — we just keep it running.
